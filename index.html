About Us

Koco & Koir Exports Pvt. Ltd was created to serve the international demand of coir products. Situated in the heart of the largest coconut growing belt in India, we aim and strive to provide quality products in the said time period.
With the confidence from our investors, we have grown rapidly while maintaining an environment friendly and cost effective approach.
If you would like to find out more about how Koco & Koir Exports Pvt. Ltd can serve you, get in touch today.


With our large infrastructure consisting of polyhouses, drying yards, washing and buffering tanks, additions to grow bag machines and a skilled labour force to match it conviniently located in one compound, we aim to provide a professional, hassle-free service to our clients.

With a combination of large inventory and the polyhouses, we aim to be able to tackle the problems the industry faces during monsoon, allowing us to live up to our commitments.


As a part of the corporate social responsibility measures, we ensure that the labors are paid fair wages, on time, prohibit the use of child labor, providing living spaces are clean and healthy, and that they have good food to eat and clean water to drink. We ensure their physical wellbeing by contacting doctors and providing the right medicines.

One stop solution for all coir products

Over the years, founders of Koco & Koir Exports Pvt. Ltd have studied the various products and their benefits. We aim to produce the products ourselves, ensuring top quality, hygiene and working standards. Today, Koco & Koir Exports Pvt. Ltd is one of the top manufacturers in the Country, offering quality products in a large variety of forms.

GO GREEN!

We are very careful with the water that we use and all water that is used is carefully treated using natural and oragnic means, and pumped into our large organic-certified farm.

We try to be energy efficient by making the most of our tropical climate to dry our products and having regular maintenance of our machines.
